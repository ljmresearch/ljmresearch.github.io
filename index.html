LJM Research Laboratory

💡 About

LJM Research Laboratory was created by a NJ Educator with a B.S. in Computer Science who wanted a dedicated space to research, experiment with, and publish findings on new and emerging technologies, outside of the traditional academic track.

By day, I am a teacher dedicated to educating students and helping them understand how the world works. The lab is the natural extension of that curiosity, a place to go deeper, ask harder questions, and share what I find with anyone who is interested.

The goal is straightforward: stay close to the leading edge of technology, do honest independent research, build software that solves real problems, and publish everything in a way that is accessible and useful for everyone, not locked behind paywalls or institutional gatekeeping.

📚 Teaching

I educate, bringing real-world context and current technology into the classroom. Teaching keeps me honest, if I can't explain something clearly, I probably don't understand it well enough yet.

💻 Research & Software

The lab is where I go deep on topics that interest me, testing new tools, writing up findings, and building small software products that put ideas into practice.

🎓 Areas of Interest

💻 Software
